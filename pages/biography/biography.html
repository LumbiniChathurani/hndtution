Meet Upul Dharmapriya, a multifaceted individual weaving the tapestry of education and expertise. The roots of his education trace back to Walala A Rathnayaka Central College, where the seeds of curiosity were planted. Beyond his formal roles, Upul is not merely an instructor but a mentor sculpting the minds of students in the realm of English medium science for grades 6 to 11. His commitment goes beyond textbooks, fostering a creative and engaging learning environment that extends the boundaries of conventional education. Step into the world of Upul Dharmapriya's tutelage, where science becomes a captivating adventure and learning transcends the ordinary.
