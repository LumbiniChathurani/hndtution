About the online Classes:

Upul Dharmapriya offers online tuition classes, primarily conducted in the evenings. Classes are available for students in grades 6 to 11, following the English medium local syllabus.

Teaching Approach:

Each lesson is accompanied by an achievement test paper to assess your understanding. Interactive discussions of the test papers take place during class time. Marks for the tests will be provided promptly, ensuring timely feedback.

Study Materials:

Tutorial materials (tutes) and test papers will be conveniently sent via WhatsApp. Students are encouraged to take printouts of the materials before the class for a more interactive learning experience.

Parent-Teacher Communication:

Parents are welcome and encouraged to engage in discussions with the teacher regarding their child's progress. A collaborative approach ensures that parents stay informed and actively participate in their child's education.

Payment Process:

The payment process is flexible and can be discussed directly with the teacher, ensuring convenience for both parties. Join Upul Dharmapriya's Online Tuition Classes for a holistic learning experience tailored to your child's needs. Your child's educational journey is just a click away!
